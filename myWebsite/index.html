    Welcome To The Golden State Academy

    The Golden State Academy is an after school program that coaches students in speech and debate. Being a competitive team, we regularly take students to tournaments both locally and across the nation. We are a nationally recognized team, winning the National's Overall School of Excellence Award in 2016. Currently, our program is operating out of three locations: Warm Springs, Evergreen & Cupertino. Our goal is that by the end of the semester students will be equipped with the ability to be confident in their communication abilities and challenge some of societies biggest issues.
